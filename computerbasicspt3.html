Document Formatting & Templates

Create professional-looking documents

Making Text Look Good

  • Font: Choose readable fonts (Arial, Times New Roman)
  • Size: 12pt for regular text, larger for titles
  • Bold: For important words or headings
  • Italic: For emphasis or book titles
  • Underline: Use sparingly
Less is more! Don't use too many different fonts or colors.

SESSION 2

Spreadsheets & Budget Tracking

Organize numbers, track money,
and let the computer do the math!

Understanding Spreadsheets

A spreadsheet is a grid made of:

Rows
Numbered
1, 2, 3, 4...
Columns
Lettered
A, B, C, D...
Cells
Each box
A1, B2, C3...
Click a cell to type in it. Press Tab or Enter to move to the next cell.

Formulas: Let the Computer Calculate!

All formulas start with an equals sign (=)

=A1+A2+A3

Or use SUM for multiple cells:

=SUM(A1:A10)
Change any number, and the total updates automatically like magic!

Common Formula Examples

  • Addition: =A1+B1+C1
  • Subtraction: =A1-B1
  • Sum of range: =SUM(A1:A5)
  • Multiple ranges: =SUM(A1:A5,C1:C5)
Practice with grocery costs or weekly expenses to get comfortable!

Building Your Personal Budget

Every budget needs:

  • Income: Social Security, pension, other
  • Expenses: Housing, food, utilities, medical
  • Totals: Use SUM formulas
  • Difference: Income minus expenses
See exactly where your money goes each month!

Digital Calendars & Organization

Never miss an appointment again!
Keep track of everything in one place.

Benefits of Digital Calendars

  • Never lose your calendar
  • Set automatic reminders
  • Share with family members
  • See everyone's schedule together
  • Access from phone and computer
  • Recurring events set once
You can still keep a paper calendar too if you like!

Adding Events to Your Calendar

  • Click on the date and time
  • Type event name (Doctor Appointment)
  • Set start and end time
  • Add location if needed
  • Add notes (doctor's phone number)
  • Click "Save"

What to Put on Your Calendar

  • Appointments: Doctor, dentist, hair salon
  • Birthdays: Family and friends
  • Medication: Daily reminder times
  • Bills: Due dates so you don't forget
  • Social: Lunch dates, club meetings
  • Weekly tasks: Trash day, water plants

Setting Up Reminders

Get notified before events:

  • 15 minutes before (for taking medicine)
  • 1 hour before (for nearby appointments)
  • 1 day before (for planning ahead)
  • Custom time (whatever you need)
Reminders pop up on your screen so you won't forget!

Repeating Events

Set once, repeats automatically:

  • Daily: Take medication
  • Weekly: Trash day, church
  • Monthly: Bill due dates
  • Yearly: Birthdays, anniversaries
Perfect for events that happen regularly!
SESSION 4

Online Banking & Account Management

Manage your money safely from home
Check balances • Pay bills • Track spending

Important: Security and safety first!

Why Use Online Banking?

  • Check balance anytime, anywhere
  • See transactions immediately
  • Pay bills without stamps or checks
  • Set up automatic payments
  • Download statements anytime
  • No need to drive to the bank
Save time, money, and gas!

Critical Safety Rules

NEVER share your password with anyone!
  • Cover screen when entering passwords
  • Log out when finished
  • Don't save passwords on shared computers
  • Use secure internet only (not public WiFi)
  • Banks NEVER ask for passwords by email or phone

Online Banking Features

View & Check
• Account balance
• Recent transactions
• Pending charges
• Download statements
Take Action
• Pay bills
• Transfer money
• Set up auto-pay
• Send money

Understanding Your Balance

  • Current Balance: Money in account now
  • Available Balance: What you can spend
  • Pending: Charges not yet completed
The available balance is usually what matters most - that's what you can actually use.

Check Your Transactions Regularly

  • Review transactions weekly
  • Look for unfamiliar charges
  • Verify all amounts are correct
  • Keep confirmation numbers
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
If something looks wrong, call your bank right away!

Tracking Subscriptions

What is a subscription?

Automatic monthly charges for services like Netflix, newspapers, magazines, streaming

  • Review subscriptions regularly
  • Cancel ones you don't use
  • Make a list with amounts and dates
  • Check your bank for recurring charges
You might be surprised what you're paying for!

Watch Out for Banking Scams

Red Flags:
  • Emails claiming your account is "locked"
  • Phone calls asking for account info
  • Text messages with urgent demands
  • Requests for passwords or PIN numbers
If in doubt: Hang up, don't click, call your bank using the number on your card!
